Simple Steak Recipe

Recently just made this steak and potato recipe with my girlfriend. Simple and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Pototos
  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Rosemary Seasoning
  • Parsely Flakes
  • Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Dice steak and potatos
  2. Heat olive oil in pan along with butter, let butter melt fully
  3. Toss potatos in pan, garlic cloves and season with rosemary, salt and pepper
  4. Leave them to cook for 4 minutes, THEN you may stir for another 5 minutes
  5. Remove potatos from pan and toss in steak in pan used for potatos. Sprinkle salt and pepper
  6. While cooking steak, juice the steak with the oil and butter in said pan. Basically bathe the steak with it
  7. Cook for approx 4 minutes or more depending on preference
  8. Toss in potatoes with steak and sprinkle parsely flakes
  9. After about 2 minutes, ENJOY!
